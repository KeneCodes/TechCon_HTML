Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for developers and has since grown into a global conference attracting thousands of tech professionals each year. The conference has expanded its reach, welcoming innovators, engineers, designers, and entrepreneurs from around the world.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower the tech community through knowledge sharing, networking, and innovation. We aim to provide a platform where passionate individuals can collaborate, inspire, and drive meaningful change in the technology landscape.
